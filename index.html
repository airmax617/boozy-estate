About This Site

Collectible Decanters, Demystified

Jim Beam's Regal China decanter program ran from 1955 to 1992 and produced over 1,500 unique designs — political figures, state themes, sports teams, vehicles, and more. They were made at the height of American bourbon culture, and they're still out there, attainable, and largely undervalued.

Boozy Estate exists to document that history, teach collectors how to navigate the market, and share a personal collection that proves you don't need to spend a fortune to own a genuine piece of American spirits history.
